### **Series: Gamers!**

**Series-wise Skills:**

1.  **Love Pentagram of Death**
    *   **Description:** A chaotic web of romantic misunderstandings erupts on the battlefield, confusing and distracting enemies.
    *   **Target:** All enemies.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** Inflicts 'Daze' on two random enemies for 1 turn. Has a 40% + (user's `lck` / 15)% chance to inflict 'Charm' (-15% `vit` and `spr`) on a third random enemy for 2 turns. All "Gamers!" allies gain +10% `lck` for 2 turns.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **Offline Meetup**
    *   **Description:** The cast gathers to hash things out, but it only leads to more chaos. Still, their attempts to communicate strengthen their resolve.
    *   **Target:** All allies.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** All "Gamers!" allies are healed for 80% of their `spr`. One random "Gamers!" ally gains +30% `atk` for 2 turns, while another random "Gamers!" ally gains +30% `int` for 2 turns.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

**Series-wise Passives:**

1.  **Escalating Misunderstandings**
    *   **Description:** The more the characters interact, the more convoluted their relationships become, creating unpredictable outcomes.
    *   **Effect:** At the start of each round after the first, there is a 20% chance for a random "Gamers!" ally and a random enemy to swap all active buffs and debuffs.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **Gamers' Synergy**
    *   **Description:** When gamers of different genres unite, their combined passion creates a powerful effect.
    *   **Effect:** If at least 3 "Gamers!" characters are in the active party, all allies gain +5% to all stats. This bonus increases to 10% if Keita, Karen, Chiaki, and Aguri are all in the 9-character deck.
    *   **Acquisition:** Active from the start of a run if the condition is met.

**Series-wise Items:**

1.  **Limited Edition Game Pre-Order**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A highly sought-after collector's edition of a new game. Its mere presence boosts morale.
    *   **Effect:** Grants all "Gamers!" characters +15% to all stats for the next battle.

2.  **Plushie from the Crane Game**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A cute plushie won after many, many attempts at the arcade. A symbol of affection and perseverance.
    *   **Effect:** Use on one character to fully restore their HP and grant them a shield equal to 25% of their max HP for the next battle.

**Series-wise Events:**

1.  **The Rooftop Confession**
    *   **Description:** Keita Amano and Karen Tendou are having another one of their famously awkward conversations on the roof. From a distance, you see Keita gesture wildly while Karen turns red. It looks like a confession, but knowing them, it could be anything.
    *   **Option A:** "Assume the worst and leave them to it." -> The misunderstanding deepens. All "Gamers!" characters start the next battle Confused (50% chance to target a random character, friend or foe, for 1 turn).
    *   **Option B:** "Go over and clarify the situation." -> You interrupt, and they explain they were just arguing about button layouts. The awkwardness is broken. The party finds a rare item.
    *   **Option C (Requires Aguri):** "Send Aguri to 'help'." -> Aguri joins the conversation and somehow makes it a hundred times more complicated. Karen and a random male character gain the 'Rivalry' buff (+15% `atk` when targeting the same enemy) for the rest of the run.

2.  **Nobe and Mono**
    *   **Description:** The party discusses their favorite indie game creators. Chiaki stays quiet, looking nervous. You know she's the creator "Nobe," and also Keita's online friend "Mono."
    *   **Option A:** "Reveal her secret." -> Chaos erupts. Chiaki is stunned for the first turn of the next battle. However, the shock inspires creativity. Chiaki gains +15 permanent `int`.
    *   **Option B:** "Keep her secret safe." -> The conversation moves on. Chiaki is relieved. She gains a +20% `spr` buff for the next 3 battles.

3.  **Opposing Viewpoints**
    *   **Description:** Keita and Chiaki get into a heated debate about whether moe characters improve or ruin video games. The argument is getting loud and derailing your progress.
    *   **Option A:** "Side with Keita's 'fun is fun' philosophy." -> Keita is emboldened. He gains the **Vigor** passive for the rest of the run. Chiaki is annoyed and suffers -10% `mag` for the next battle.
    *   **Option B:** "Side with Chiaki's 'gameplay first' stance." -> Chiaki feels validated. She gains the **Focus Magic** skill for the rest of the run. Keita is dejected and suffers -10% `atk` for the next battle.
    *   **Option C:** "Tell them both to shut up." -> They are both shocked into silence. The party gets to skip the next normal battle node.

4.  **Aguri's Gambit (Co-op Event: Aguri & Karen)**
    *   **Description:** Aguri, convinced her boyfriend is cheating, drags Karen into an elaborate and poorly-thought-out spy mission. They're about to be discovered.
    *   **Option A:** "Create a diversion." -> You successfully draw attention away. Aguri and Karen's bond as "allies" deepens. Both characters gain +10 permanent `lck`.
    *   **Option B:** "Let them get caught." -> They are caught and thoroughly embarrassed. Both characters start the next battle with the 'Daze' debuff for 1 turn.

**Equipments:**

1.  **Tasuku's Phone**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** The phone of a popular guy, filled with confusing messages and half-formed plans to help his friends.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** Grants the **Opportunist** passive (Deals 15% more damage to debuffed enemies).

2.  **Chiaki's Controller**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A well-worn but perfectly maintained controller. The tool of a true, focused gamer.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer gains **Magic Up II** (+20% `mag`). If equipped by Chiaki Hoshinomori, her skill cooldowns are reduced by 1 at the start of battle.

3.  **Aguri's Cat Plushie**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A prize from a crane game, a symbol of a surprisingly resilient and pure-hearted relationship.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** Grants the **Soothing Presence** passive (All allies receive 10% more healing). If equipped by Aguri, she also grants a 5% `lck` bonus to all allies.

### **Character: Karen Tendou (3 Star)**

**Skills/Passives:**

*   **3★ (Base):**
    *   **Signature Skill: Idol's Charm**
        *   **Description:** As the school's perfect idol, Karen flashes a dazzling smile that incapacitates a foe, leaving them open to attack.
        *   **Target:** The enemy with the highest `atk`.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `int`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Deals 80% of `mag` as light damage. Has a 40% + (user's `lck`/15)% chance to 'Stun' the target for 1 turn.
        *   **Cooldown:** 4 turns
    *   **Normal Skill 1:** Rally Cry
    *   **Normal Skill 2:** Vicious Strike
    *   **Signature Passive 1: Golden Girl**
        *   **Description:** Karen's reputation as the perfect student precedes her, granting her an advantageous start.
        *   **Effect:** At the start of battle, Karen gains +15% to all stats for 2 turns.
    *   **Signature Passive 2: Stalker-in-Love**
        *   **Description:** Her obsession with a certain someone leads her to act erratically, making her attacks surprisingly unpredictable.
        *   **Effect:** When Karen's HP drops below 70%, her skills have a 15% chance to target a random enemy instead of their intended target, but deal 20% more damage if they do.

*   **4★ (Awakened):**
    *   **Ultimate Skill: "Yes, Gladly!"**
        *   **Description:** Misunderstanding a comment as a heartfelt confession, Karen is overcome with joy, unleashing a powerful and supportive wave of energy.
        *   **Activation Condition:** Can only be used after an ally has used a buffing skill on Karen.
        *   **Target:** All allies.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `lck`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Heals all allies for 120% of `spr`. All allies gain +20% `atk`, `mag`, and `spd` for 2 turns.
        *   **Cooldown:** 8 turns (Once per battle).

*   **5★ (Max Awakened):**
    *   **Signature Skill 2: Competitive Spirit**
        *   **Description:** Karen turns the battle into a high-stakes competition, singling out the strongest opponent to prove her own superiority.
        *   **Target:** The enemy with the highest combined stats.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `atk`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Deals 140% of `atk` as physical damage. Forces the target to attack Karen for 1 turn. Karen gains +30% `vit` for that turn.
        *   **Cooldown:** 4 turns

**Exclusive Events:**

1.  **A Crumbling Facade**
    *   **Description:** You find Karen slumped over in the game club room, muttering to herself after another confusing encounter with Keita. Her perfect idol persona is completely gone, replaced by a flustered, obsessive mess.
    *   **Option A:** "Offer her some rational advice." -> She tries to listen, but twists your words into more romantic delusions. She gains +10 permanent `int`, but also the **Unpredictable** passive for the next 3 battles.
    *   **Option B:** "Just agree that Keita is amazing." -> She lights up, her energy completely restored. Karen gains a permanent +10% `atk` and `mag` for the rest of the run.

2.  **The Game Club Challenge**
    *   **Description:** To attract new members, Karen sets up a public challenge for a fighting game. She is undefeated, but her competitive intensity is scaring potential recruits away.
    *   **Option A:** "Challenge her and let her win." -> Her ego is stoked. She gains the **Commander's Aura** passive for the next 3 battles.
    *   **Option B:** "Challenge her and actually try to win." -> A fierce battle ensues. If your team's total `lck` is higher than Karen's, you win. The shock of defeat humbles her. She gains +15 permanent `vit`. If you lose, she becomes overconfident, gaining +15% `atk` but -15% `vit` for the next battle.

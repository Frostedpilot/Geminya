### **Series: Magia Record: Puella Magi Madoka Magica Gaiden**

**Series-wise Skills:**

1.  **Doppel Manifestation**
    *   **Description:** A Magical Girl from Kamihama unleashes her Doppel, a controlled manifestation of her Witch form, unleashing devastating but corrupting power.
    *   **Target:** All enemies.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** Deals 120% of the user's `mag` as dark damage to all enemies. The user then becomes inflicted with 'Corruption' for 2 turns (reduces `spr` by 30% and takes 10% of max HP as damage per turn). This skill's cooldown is shared among all "Magia Record" characters.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **Connect**
    *   **Description:** The Magical Girls of Kamihama link their magic, sharing their strength to empower an ally.
    *   **Target:** An ally with the highest `atk` or `mag`.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** The target ally receives a buff based on the user's highest Role Potency. (Attacker/Mage: +25% `atk`/`mag` for 2 turns. Healer/Buffer: +25% `spr`/`int` for 2 turns. Defender/Debuffer: Grants a shield for 150% of user's `vit`).
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

**Series-wise Passives:**

1.  **Kamihama's Anomaly**
    *   **Description:** The strange properties of Kamihama City prevent a Magical Girl's Soul Gem from being fully tainted, allowing them to manifest Doppels instead of becoming Witches.
    *   **Effect:** The first time a "Magia Record" ally receives fatal damage, they survive with 1 HP and the cooldown of their **Doppel Manifestation** skill (if acquired) is reset. This effect can only trigger once per character per battle.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **Wings of Magius's Salvation**
    *   **Description:** The promise of eternal freedom from their fate empowers the Magical Girls, but at a cost to their individual will.
    *   **Effect:** As long as there are 4 or more "Magia Record" characters in the party, all "Magia Record" characters gain +10% `mag` and `atk`, but have their `lck` reduced by 10%.
    *   **Acquisition:** Active from the start of a run if the condition is met.

**Series-wise Items:**

1.  **Grief Seed Fragment**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A shard of a crystallized Witch's curse. Using it purifies a small amount of corruption from a Soul Gem.
    *   **Effect:** Heals a single character for 30% of their max HP. Can be used in or out of combat, but using it in combat consumes a turn.

2.  **Magia Stone**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A stone pulsating with magical energy, used to enhance a Magical Girl's power.
    *   **Effect:** Use before a battle to grant a single "Magia Record" character +15% to all stats for that battle.

**Series-wise Events:**

1.  **The Rumor of the Seance Shrine**
    *   **Description:** The team hears whispers of a shrine where one can meet the souls of the dead. It's one of the Uwasa spreading through Kamihama. Yachiyo is wary, but Iroha, thinking of Ui, wants to investigate.
    *   **Option A:** "Investigate the Rumor." -> You face a battle against unusual enemies. Upon winning, you encounter the Uwasa. Iroha Tamaki gains +10 permanent `spr`, but the entire party takes 15% HP damage from the unsettling experience.
    *   **Option B:** "It's too dangerous. Avoid it." -> You steer clear of the shrine. The party is safe, but the missed opportunity weighs on them. All "Magia Record" allies gain -5% `lck` for the next 2 battles.

    *   **Description:** A magical girl wearing a black hood approaches, offering salvation from the cruel fate of all magical girls. She invites you to join the Wings of Magius.
    *   **Option A:** "Accept their 'salvation'." -> The party gains the powerful **Doppel Manifestation** series skill, but their resolve is shaken. A random ally has their `spr` permanently reduced by 10% for the rest of the run.
    *   **Option B:** "Refuse and fight for your own hope." -> The hooded girl leaves peacefully, but her words linger. The party gains +15% debuff resistance for the next 3 battles.

    *   **Option B:** "Respect Yachiyo's silence." -> Yachiyo retreats further into her shell. She gains the **Lone Wolf** passive for the next 3 battles, but Iroha is saddened, receiving a -5% `spr` debuff for the next battle.

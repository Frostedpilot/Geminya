### **Series: Nukitashi the Animation**

**Series-wise Skills:**

1.  **NLNS Assemble!**
    *   **Description:** The members of "No Love No Sex" coordinate a daring operation, disrupting the enemy's formation and exposing their weaknesses.
    *   **Target:** All enemies.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** Pushes all enemies in the front row to the back row and pulls all enemies in the back row to the front. Applies a -15% `vit` and `spr` debuff to all enemies for 2 turns.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **Island Preservation Law Repeal**
    *   **Description:** A passionate speech that rallies the team to fight for their freedom, granting them immense resolve.
    *   **Target:** All allies.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** Cleanses all debuffs from all allies. All "Nukitashi the Animation" allies gain +20% to all stats for 3 turns.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

**Series-wise Passives:**

1.  **Secret Society's Bond**
    *   **Description:** The trust between the members of NLNS allows them to protect each other from harm.
    *   **Effect:** Once per turn, when a "Nukitashi the Animation" ally drops below 30% HP, the ally from this series with the highest current HP grants them a shield equal to 150% of their `spr`.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **Island Fugitives**
    *   **Description:** Constantly on the run from the island's authorities, the NLNS members have become adept at staying hidden and striking from the shadows.
    *   **Effect:** All "Nukitashi the Animation" allies gain +15% `spd` for the first 2 rounds of combat. While in the back row, they generate 25% less threat.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

**Series-wise Items:**

1.  **Handmade Croquettes**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A delicious, perfectly fried croquette made by Hinami. A taste of simple, everyday happiness.
    *   **Effect:** Restores 40% HP to a single ally and grants them the 'Well-Fed' buff, increasing their `vit` and `spr` by 10% for the next battle.

2.  **Anti-Island Preservation Law Flyer**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A crudely drawn but passionate flyer denouncing the island's bizarre laws. Surprisingly effective.
    *   **Effect:** Use before a battle to inflict -10% `atk` and `mag` on all enemies for the first turn.

**Series-wise Events:**

1.  **The Secret Hideout**
    *   **Description:** The NLNS hideout has been compromised! You need to find a new base of operations, fast. Hinami knows a safe, abandoned warehouse, but Nanase insists on a more defensible cave system.
    *   **Option A:** "Trust Hinami's choice." -> The warehouse is comfortable. The party is well-rested, and all allies recover 25% of their max HP.
    *   **Option B:** "Go with Nanase's tactical decision." -> The cave is damp and cold, but secure. All "Nukitashi the Animation" characters gain a permanent +5% `vit` for the run.

2.  **Misaki's Mischief**
    *   **Description:** Misaki has "acquired" a large batch of fireworks and wants to set them off as a diversion. Asane worries it will draw too much attention.
    *   **Option A:** "Let the fireworks fly!" -> The spectacular display causes a huge commotion. You skip the next 2 non-boss nodes on the map, but the next boss will start the battle with a +10% `atk` buff.
    *   **Option B:** "Listen to Asane's caution." -> The party lays low. Asane is relieved, granting all allies the **Clarity** passive for the next battle.

3.  **A Moral Dilemma**
    *   **Description:** You capture a low-ranking member of the island's preservation committee. Nanase wants to interrogate her forcefully, but Hinami believes they can win her over with kindness.
    *   **Option A (Requires Nanase):** "A necessary evil." -> Nanase learns critical information. You gain a rare item, but Nanase's `spr` is permanently reduced by 10 for the run.
    *   **Option B (Requires Hinami):** "The gentle approach." -> Hinami's kindness wins the guard over. She joins your party as a temporary 1-star "Ally" character for the next 3 battles.

4.  **The Group's Mascot (Co-op Event: Nanase & Misaki)**
    *   **Description:** Misaki has "designed" a new mascot for NLNS, a bizarre, vaguely phallic creature. She proudly shows it to Nanase, who is utterly appalled and demands a more dignified symbol.
    *   **Option A:** "Praise Misaki's creativity." -> Misaki is ecstatic. She gains +15 permanent `lck`. Nanase starts the next battle with the 'Anxiety' debuff for 2 turns.
    *   **Option B:** "Agree with Nanase's concerns." -> Nanase feels validated. She gains +10 permanent `vit`. Misaki pouts and her `mag` is reduced by 10% for the next battle.

**Equipments:**

1.  **NLNS Armband**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A simple armband worn by members of NLNS. It's a symbol of their rebellion and unity.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer gains the **Series Pride** passive (+5% to all stats for each other ally from the same Series).

2.  **Hinami's Apron**
    *   **Type:** Armor
    *   **Description:** An apron filled with love, and probably some flour. It has a strangely comforting effect.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer gains the **Medic's Intuition** passive (The ally with the lowest max HP starts the battle with a 'Regeneration' effect for 3 turns).

3.  **Nanase's Kendo Sword**
    *   **Type:** Weapon
    *   **Description:** A well-used shinai. It's not a real sword, but in Nanase's hands, it's just as intimidating.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer gains the **Frontline Commander** passive (+10% `atk` and `vit` in the front row). If equipped by Nanase Katagiri, she also gains +10% `spd`.

4.  **Misaki's Hacking Terminal**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A small, custom-built terminal used for information gathering and general chaos.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer's debuff skills have a 25% chance to reduce the target's `spr` by 10% for 2 turns. If equipped by Misaki Hotori, this effect also reduces `int`.

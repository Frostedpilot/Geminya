### **Series: Ore wo Suki nano wa Omae dake ka yo**

**Series-wise Skills:**

1.  **Are You the Only One Who Loves Me?**
    *   **Description:** The user calls out the hypocrisy of their foes, exposing their weaknesses and turning their strengths against them.
    *   **Target:** All enemies.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** Deals 50% `mag` as dark damage to all enemies. For each buff on a target, this skill deals an additional 15% `mag` damage and has a 25% chance to remove that buff.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **Bench-kun's Counsel**
    *   **Description:** A moment of quiet reflection clarifies the user's mind, preparing them for the coming conflict and offering aid to their truest ally.
    *   **Target:** Self and the ally with the highest `lck`.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** User removes 2 debuffs from themself and gains +20% `int` for 3 turns. The ally with the highest `lck` is healed for 100% of the user's `spr`.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

**Series-wise Passives:**

1.  **Two-Faced Friendship**
    *   **Description:** The bonds within this group are... complicated. This uncertainty can lead to surprising outcomes, for better or worse.
    *   **Effect:** At the start of battle, all "Ore wo Suki nano wa Omae dake ka yo" allies gain +10% to a random stat for 3 turns. However, at the end of each of their turns, they have a 5% chance to also apply a -5% debuff to the same stat on an adjacent ally for 1 turn.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **The Lone Observer**
    *   **Description:** Standing apart from the drama allows for a clearer view of the battlefield.
    *   **Effect:** If there is only one "Ore wo Suki nano wa Omae dake ka yo" character on the team, that character gains +15% `int` and `lck`.
    *   **Acquisition:** Active from the start of a run if the condition is met.

**Series-wise Items:**

1.  **Pansy's Secret Library Key**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A key to a hidden corner of the library where the truth can be found. Use before a battle to gain a significant tactical advantage.
    *   **Effect:** For the next battle, all allies gain 'Clarity' (reduces duration of mental debuffs by 1 turn) and their attacks ignore 15% of the enemy's `vit` and `spr`.

2.  **Sun-chan's Signed Baseball**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A baseball signed by the ace of the baseball team. Its presence is surprisingly motivating.
    *   **Effect:** Grants one ally +20% `atk` and `spd` for their next battle.

**Series-wise Events:**

1.  **A "Friendly" Favor**
    *   **Description:** Himawari and Cosmos approach you with a sweet smile, asking for a "small favor" that seems designed to benefit them at your expense. They promise they'll make it up to you.
    *   **Option A:** "Help them out." -> You lose a significant amount of currency. In the next battle, Himawari and Cosmos both start with a +15% `lck` buff.
    *   **Option B:** "Politely refuse." -> They seem disappointed but understand. The party gains the "Debuff Resistance" passive for the next 2 battles.

2.  **The Grandstand Play**
    *   **Description:** Sun-chan is in a slump during a critical baseball game. He's trying too hard to look cool and it's backfiring. What advice do you give him?
    *   **Option A:** "Tell him to be himself." -> Sun-chan relaxes and hits a home run. The party finds a rare piece of equipment after the game.
    *   **Option B:** "Tell him to show off even more." -> He strikes out spectacularly. He gains the 'Anxiety' debuff for the next battle, reducing his `int` and `spr` by 20%.

3.  **Pansy's True Face (Co-op Event: Sumireko & any other character)**
    *   **Description:** While alone, Sumireko (Pansy) drops her "gloomy" persona, revealing a sharp, cynical, and surprisingly charming side. Another ally happens to walk by and see this.
    *   **Option A:** "Pretend you saw nothing." -> Sumireko is grateful. She gains +10 permanent `lck` for the run.
    *   **Option B:** "Confront her about it." -> Sumireko is annoyed but intrigued by your boldness. She gains +10 permanent `int`, but the other ally starts the next battle with -10% `spd` due to their confusion.

4.  **The Library Stare-Down**
    *   **Description:** You enter the library to find Pansy and Asunaro staring intently at each other from across a table, a silent battle of wits unfolding. You feel an immense pressure in the air.
    *   **Requirement:** Have Sumireko (Pansy) and Hina (Asunaro) in your party.
    *   **Option A:** "Interject with a stupid joke." -> You break the tension. Both characters are annoyed, giving them -5% to all stats in the next battle. However, you find a valuable item they left behind in their haste to leave.
    *   **Option B:** "Quietly back away." -> The tension continues to build. Both characters gain +15% `mag` and `int` for the next 3 battles.

**Equipments:**

1.  **The Bench of Solitude**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A surprisingly comfortable bench. It's the perfect place for introspection and plotting against your so-called friends.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer gains the **End-of-Turn Heal** passive (recovers 3% max HP after acting). If the wearer is the only character from their series on the team, this healing is tripled.

2.  **Cosmos's Watering Can**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A cute watering can used by the student council's flower expert. It seems to overflow with sweet poison.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer's debuffing skills have a 25% chance to also apply a 'Regeneration' effect (20% of user's `spr` per turn) to the ally with the lowest HP for 2 turns. If equipped by Sakura Akino, her `lck` is increased by 15%.

3.  **Tsubaki's Camera**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A professional-grade camera used by the ace of the newspaper club. Perfect for exposing the truth.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** At the start of battle, applies 'Exposed' (-10% `vit` and `spr` for 3 turns) to a random enemy in the back row.

4.  **Sasanqua's Towel**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A simple towel, always ready to help someone in need, no matter the user's own feelings on the matter.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer gains the **Selfless Protector** passive (Once per battle, when an adjacent ally drops below 50% HP, grant them a shield equal to 100% of the wearer's `spr`).

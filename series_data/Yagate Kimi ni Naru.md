### **Series: Yagate Kimi ni Naru**

**Series-wise Skills:**

1.  **Student Council Stage Play**
    *   **Description:** The student council members perform their heartfelt stage play, inspiring the entire team with their dedication and emotion.
    *   **Target:** All allies.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** Grants all "Yagate Kimi ni Naru" allies a buff that increases their highest offensive stat (`atk` or `mag`) and highest defensive stat (`vit` or `spr`) by 25% for 3 turns. Also provides a moderate heal over time, restoring 30% of the user's `spr` for 2 turns.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through the series event "The Script's Final Scene".

2.  **Unspoken Understanding**
    *   **Description:** Two allies share a moment of profound, unspoken connection, anticipating each other's needs perfectly.
    *   **Target:** The two "Yagate Kimi ni Naru" allies with the lowest current HP percentages.
    *   **Effect Breakdown:** Both targets' HP percentages are equalized. Then, both targets are granted a shield equal to 150% of the user's `spr` for 2 turns and have their skill cooldowns reduced by 1.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

**Series-wise Passives:**

1.  **Bloom Into You**
    *   **Description:** As characters from this series fight alongside each other, they grow to understand themselves and each other, unlocking new potential.
    *   **Effect:** For every 2 turns that a "Yagate Kimi ni Naru" character is on the field, they gain a permanent +2% to `int` and `spr` for the rest of the battle. This effect stacks up to 5 times.
    *   **Acquisition:** Acquired through a specific series event during a run.

2.  **Facade of Perfection**
    *   **Description:** The pressure to be perfect creates a fragile but effective defense.
    *   **Effect:** At the start of battle, if there are 3 or more "Yagate Kimi ni Naru" allies, the one with the highest combined stats gains 'Death Defiance' (survives the next fatal blow with 1 HP). However, if that character is the last one remaining from the series, they gain 'Anxiety' (-15% `int` and `spr`) for the rest of the battle.
    *   **Acquisition:** Active from the start of a run if the condition is met.

**Series-wise Items:**

1.  **Koyomi's First Draft**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A draft of the student council play script, filled with heartfelt emotion and raw potential. Use before a battle for inspiration.
    *   **Effect:** Grants all "Yagate Kimi ni Naru" characters +20% `int` for the next battle.

2.  **Aquarium Ticket Stubs**
    *   **Type:** Consumable
    *   **Description:** A pair of ticket stubs from a memorable date at the aquarium. Use outside of battle to reminisce.
    *   **Effect:** Fully restores the HP of two "Yagate Kimi ni Naru" characters and grants them +10% `spr` for the next battle.

**Series-wise Events:**

1.  **The Script's Final Scene**
    *   **Description:** Koyomi presents the final scene of the student council play script to the group. Yuu feels that the ending, where the main character loses her memories, doesn't feel right for Touko. Touko, however, seems adamant about performing it as is.
    *   **Option A:** "Convince Touko to change the script." -> The play becomes a story of self-acceptance. The party gains the **Student Council Stage Play** series skill. Touko and Yuu gain +10 permanent `spr`.
    *   **Option B:** "Respect Touko's wishes and perform the original." -> The play is a tragic masterpiece, but leaves a lingering sadness. The party gains a rare, powerful offensive item, but Touko starts the next 3 battles with a -10% `spr` debuff for 2 turns.

2.  **Choosing the Right Words**
    *   **Description:** While studying, Yuu struggles to understand her own feelings, finding the words in books and songs don't match her experience. Sayaka, observing from a distance, seems to understand the conflict all too well.
    *   **Option A:** "Encourage Yuu to talk it over with Sayaka." -> Sayaka offers quiet, insightful advice. Yuu gains the **Fast Learner** passive for the rest of the run. Sayaka gains +10 permanent `int`.
    *   **Option B:** "Let Yuu work it out on her own." -> Yuu remains conflicted. She starts the next battle 'Silenced' for 1 turn, but gains +15 permanent `spr` from the experience.

3.  **A Cafe Respite**
    *   **Description:** The student council decides to take a break at a local cafe run by Miyako Kodama. Miyako offers some friendly advice and a listening ear to the students' troubles.
    *   **Option A:** "Order the special parfait." -> The delicious treat lifts everyone's spirits. All allies are fully healed and gain +10% `lck` for the next battle.
    *   **Option B:** "Ask Miyako for life advice." -> Miyako's mature perspective is refreshing. One random "Yagate Kimi ni Naru" character gains the **Soothing Presence** passive for the rest of the run.

4.  **Unspoken Support (Co-op Event: Yuu & Touko)**
    *   **Description:** Touko is pushing herself too hard for the student council election, trying to be the perfect candidate her sister was. Yuu notices she's exhausted but knows Touko won't admit it.
    *   **Option A:** "Quietly take on some of her tasks." -> Touko, though she doesn't say it, is visibly relieved. Yuu gains the **Selfless Soul** passive for the next 3 battles. Touko gains +10% max HP for the run.
    *   **Option B:** "Confront her directly about overworking herself." -> Touko becomes defensive and pushes Yuu away emotionally. Both characters start the next battle with -10% `vit` for 2 turns.

**Equipments:**

1.  **Yuu's Star-Shaped Pin**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A small star-shaped pin that Yuu wears. A simple but constant reminder of a special connection.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer's buffing skills have a 20% chance to also grant the target 'Regeneration' for 1 turn (30% `spr`). If equipped by Yuu Koito, this chance increases to 40%.

2.  **Touko's Student Council Badge**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A badge signifying the president of the student council. It carries the weight of great expectations.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** Grants the **Commander's Aura** passive (+5% `atk` to all allies). If equipped by Touko Nanami, she also gains the **Aggressor** passive (+50% threat).

3.  **Sayaka's Bookmark**
    *   **Type:** Accessory
    *   **Description:** A well-worn bookmark from a favorite book. It represents quiet contemplation and unwavering devotion.
    *   **Effect (Passive):** The wearer gains +15% `spr`. If the wearer uses a single-target shield or heal on an ally, the wearer also gains +10% `vit` for 1 turn. If equipped by Sayaka Saeki, the `vit` bonus is doubled.

### **Character: Touko Nanami (3 Star)**

**Skills/Passives:**

*   **3★ (Base):**
    *   **Signature Skill: Perfect Facade**
        *   **Description:** Touko presents herself as the perfect student council president, inspiring an ally but putting immense pressure on herself.
        *   **Target:** The ally with the highest `int`.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `int`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Grants the target +25% to all stats for 2 turns. Touko receives a 'Reflect' buff for 1 turn, but also receives 'Anxiety' (-15% `int` and `spr`) for 2 turns.
        *   **Cooldown:** 4 turns
    *   **Normal Skill 1:** Rally Cry
    *   **Normal Skill 2:** Holy Smite
    *   **Signature Passive 1: Burden of Expectation**
        *   **Description:** Touko's drive to become like her late sister makes her powerful, but vulnerable to those who see through her act.
        *   **Effect:** Touko gains +15% to all stats as long as she is above 80% HP. If her HP drops below 80%, this bonus is lost for the rest of the battle. If Yuu Koito is an ally, this threshold is lowered to 50% HP.
    *   **Signature Passive 2: Unacceptable Love**
        *   **Description:** Touko cannot accept love from anyone who loves the "perfect" version of her, as she herself despises her true, weaker self.
        *   **Effect:** Touko takes 15% less damage from enemies, but also receives 15% less healing from allies other than Yuu Koito.

*   **4★ (Awakened):**
    *   **Ultimate Skill: Please Don't Fall In Love With Me**
        *   **Description:** Touko's desperate plea to Yuu, allowing her to be vulnerable without the fear of being loved for a false image. This vulnerability becomes a unique source of strength.
        *   **Activation Condition:** Can only be used when Yuu Koito is an active ally and Touko is below 50% HP.
        *   **Target:** Self.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `int`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Touko removes all debuffs from herself and becomes 'Untargetable' for 1 turn. For the next 2 turns, all of Touko's skills will target Yuu Koito with a supportive version of the skill (e.g., a damaging skill becomes a heal, a debuff becomes a buff of equivalent power).
        *   **Cooldown:** 7 turns (Once per battle).

*   **5★ (Max Awakened):**
    *   **Signature Skill 2: I'm Okay Being Me**
        *   **Description:** After the play, Touko begins to accept all parts of herself, turning her former weakness into a source of inspiration for everyone.
        *   **Target:** All allies.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `spr`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Heals all allies for 80% of `spr` and grants all allies a buff that converts 20% of their `spr` into `atk` and `mag` for 3 turns.
        *   **Cooldown:** 5 turns

**Exclusive Events:**

1.  **A Sudden Confession**
    *   **Description:** After you help her reject a suitor, Touko suddenly confesses to you, catching you completely off-guard. She says she likes you precisely because you won't fall in love with her, a statement that feels both intimate and distant.
    *   **Option A:** "Accept her feelings, on her terms." -> Touko seems relieved. She gains the **Series Pride** passive for the next 3 battles.
    *   **Option B:** "Question her definition of 'love'." -> Touko is taken aback and becomes withdrawn. She gains +10 permanent `int` but starts the next battle with -15% `lck` for 2 turns.

2.  **The Weight of a Role**
    *   **Description:** You find Touko practicing her lines for the student council play late at night. She's pushing herself to the point of exhaustion, trying to perfectly embody a role that mirrors her own internal struggles.
    *   **Option A:** "Help her practice and offer support." -> Your presence helps her relax and find new meaning in the role. Touko gains +15 permanent `spr`.
    *   **Option B:** "Tell her she needs to rest." -> She reluctantly agrees but feels she has fallen short of her own expectations. Touko gains the **Setup** passive for the next 3 battles.

### **Character: Yuu Koito (3 Star)**

**Skills/Passives:**

*   **3★ (Base):**
    *   **Signature Skill: Indecisive Support**
        *   **Description:** Unable to commit to a single course of action, Yuu provides broad but unfocused support to her allies.
        *   **Target:** All allies.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `int`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Grants all allies one random buff ('atk' up, 'mag' up, 'vit' up, 'spr' up) for 2 turns.
        *   **Cooldown:** 3 turns
    *   **Normal Skill 1:** First Aid
    *   **Normal Skill 2:** Haste
    *   **Signature Passive 1: Can't Feel "Special"**
        *   **Description:** Yuu's inability to feel the "spark" of love makes her immune to romantic manipulation and oddly perceptive.
        *   **Effect:** Yuu is immune to 'Charm' and 'Daze'. When an ally is afflicted with 'Charm', Yuu's next single-target skill will automatically target that ally and cleanse the effect.
    *   **Signature Passive 2: Observant Empathy**
        *   **Description:** Though she doesn't feel love herself, Yuu is adept at observing and understanding the feelings of others, allowing her to provide the right kind of help.
        *   **Effect:** At the start of her turn, if an ally has a debuff, Yuu's `spr` is increased by 20% for that turn. If an ally has a buff, Yuu's `int` is increased by 20% for that turn.

*   **4★ (Awakened):**
    *   **Ultimate Skill: I Think I Can Fall For You**
        *   **Description:** In a moment of clarity, Yuu finally chooses her own path and decides what her feelings mean to her, providing definitive and powerful support to the one she cares for.
        *   **Activation Condition:** Can only be used when Touko Nanami is an active ally.
        *   **Target:** Touko Nanami.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `spr`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Fully heals Touko, cleanses all debuffs from her, and grants her the **Vigor** passive (+20% `atk` and `mag` while at 100% HP) for the rest of the battle. Yuu gains +30% `int` and `spr` for the rest of the battle.
        *   **Cooldown:** 8 turns (Once per battle).

*   **5★ (Max Awakened):**
    *   **Signature Skill 2: I'll Handle This**
        *   **Description:** Stepping up as the student council campaign manager, Yuu takes decisive action to protect an ally from harm and set them up for success.
        *   **Target:** The ally with the most debuffs.
        *   **Scaling Stat:** `int`
        *   **Effect Breakdown:** Cleanses all debuffs from the target and grants them a shield that absorbs damage equal to 200% of Yuu's `int`. The target's action bar is filled by 30%.
        *   **Cooldown:** 5 turns

**Exclusive Events:**

1.  **Shoujo Manga Troubles**
    *   **Description:** You find Yuu in her family's bookstore, staring intently at a shoujo manga. She sighs, confessing that she loves the stories but can't understand the feelings the characters experience.
    *   **Option A:** "Tell her it's okay not to feel the same way." -> Yuu seems comforted by your words. She gains +10 permanent `spr`.
    *   **Option B:** "Recommend a different genre." -> She tries a mystery novel instead and gets engrossed. She gains +10 permanent `int`.

2.  **An Unexpected Request**
    *   **Description:** A boy from another class confesses to Yuu. Still unsure how to handle these situations, she asks for your advice on how to let him down gently.
    *   **Option A:** "Help her rehearse a kind rejection." -> The rejection goes smoothly. The party gains a small amount of currency, and Yuu gains the **Soothing Presence** passive for the next battle.
    *   **Option B:** "Tell her to be brutally honest." -> It's awkward and messy, but ultimately effective. Yuu starts the next battle with +20% `lck` for 3 turns.
